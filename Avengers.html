Avengers: Infinity War

About

Avengers: Infinity War is a 2018 superhero film produced by Marvel Studios and directed by Anthony and Joe Russo. The movie features an ensemble cast of superheroes, including Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow, Hulk, Spider-Man, and Doctor Strange. The plot revolves around their efforts to stop the supervillain Thanos from collecting the six Infinity Stones, granting him unlimited power to wipe out half of all life in the universe. The film was a massive commercial and critical success, grossing over $2 billion worldwide. Avengers: Infinity War is a thrilling and emotional superhero film that successfully combines action, drama, and humor.
